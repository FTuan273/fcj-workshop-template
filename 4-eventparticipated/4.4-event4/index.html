STUDY TOUR: AWS — ENTERPRISE CLOUD ARCHITECTURES & INDUSTRY APPLICATIONS Report (04/07/2026)

Event Purpose

  • Market Update and Career Orientation: Provide an overview of recruitment trends in the Cloud Computing field and outline career development paths on a global scale.
  • Narrowing the “Theory and Practice” Gap: Guide how to transform theoretical knowledge from the classroom into practical technological solutions, helping candidates score absolute points with employers.
  • Sharing Practical Experience from Experts: Bring authentic perspectives on the journey of transforming from students to data engineers, and analyze how enterprises optimize real-world data architectures led by top experts from AWS, Renova Cloud, and Cloud Kinetics.
  • Inspiring and Nurturing Talent: In-depth discussions (via Fireside Chat) on strategies to nurture and develop the next generation of breakthrough tech talents in Vietnam.

Event Information

  • Event Name: STUDY TOUR: AWS — ENTERPRISE CLOUD ARCHITECTURES & INDUSTRY APPLICATIONS
  • Date: 04/07/2026
  • Location: 26th Floor - AWS Vietnam Office (Bitexco Tower, 2 Hai Trieu Street, Saigon Ward, Ho Chi Minh City)
  • Role: Attendee
  • Event Photos: Facebook Link

Speakers / Sessions

1. Mr. Nguyen Gia Hung — Job Market Trends & Career Trends

Mr. Nguyen Gia Hung brought an overview of the job market and the latest career trends today. The session focused on updating prominent recruitment trends in the Cloud Computing field, while outlining global career development paths. In particular, he shared an inspiring story about the journey “from the lecture hall to the battlefield” — the process of transforming from a student to a real data engineer, along with deep insights into how businesses optimize data architecture in a real environment.

2. Ms. Nhu Tran — Survival Skills & Workplace Culture

Not stopping at professional knowledge, the event also equipped attendees with essential soft skills through the lens of Ms. Nhu Tran.

3. Mr. Khang Nguyen — AI Ready Freshers

AI-Ready Freshers: An inspiring sharing session combining the practical learning journey and career orientation in the AI era. Mr. Khang Nguyen emphasized equipping an agile mindset, technology mastery skills, and quick adaptability to help freshers confidently break through and score points with employers.

4. Mr. Vinh Banh — Renova Cloud, Cloud Kinetics

The sharing session focused on core knowledge such as programming, databases, data structures and algorithms, distributed systems, APIs, and necessary technology platforms before learning Cloud & AI. In addition, Mr. Vinh Banh introduced the skill development roadmap for Cloud engineers and how to transform fundamental knowledge into practical Cloud applications.

Group Photo

Key Highlights

  • Overview of Cloud & Data: Mr. Nguyen Gia Hung’s sharing brought a realistic view of the job market, global recruitment trends, and the transformation path from student to a real Data Engineer.
  • Office Survival Skills: Ms. Nhu Tran equipped attendees with Survival Skills and how to understand corporate culture, helping students become more confident and tactful when entering a professional working environment.
  • “AI-Ready Freshers” Mindset: Mr. Khang Nguyen guided how to build an agile mindset, technology mastery skills, and quick adaptability to leverage the power of AI, thereby creating a great competitive advantage in the eyes of employers.
  • Transforming Foundation into Cloud Architecture: Mr. Vinh Banh emphasized the importance of core knowledge (Databases, APIs, Distributed Systems, Programming) and guided how to assemble these “bricks” into enterprise-scale Cloud solutions (Enterprise Architectures).

Takeaways & Application

  • Optimizing Practical Project Architecture: Understand the importance of mastering foundational knowledge before deploying Cloud. The knowledge of distributed systems and API design from the event will be directly applied to optimizing databases and backend processing flows (using Node.js, Java Spring Boot) for ongoing projects like the WebFood platform, ensuring the system is resilient and easy to scale.
  • Integrating AI Mindset into Software Development: Apply the “AI-Ready” mindset by constantly updating new technologies, researching the integration of AI solutions (like Google Gemini) into web/app projects to improve chatbot features, enhance user experience, and create technical highlights.
  • Equipping Soft Skills and Work Discipline: Apply “survival skills” and office culture to improve team communication skills. At the same time, maintain strict discipline in source code management, task division on GitHub/GitLab to get used to enterprise-standard work styles right from school.

Event Experience

The event took place in the professional space of the AWS Vietnam office (Bitexco Tower) and truly brought an overwhelming and inspiring experience. Listening to the “from lecture hall to battlefield” stories of the speakers not only helped bridge the gap between theory and practice but also provided a very clear career path. The open, dynamic atmosphere at the event added tremendous motivation to constantly hone programming skills, ready for the challenges in the volatile world of Cloud Computing and AI.

The STUDY TOUR: AWS (04/07/2026 at Bitexco) event is a bridge to help students transform classroom knowledge into practical combat capability. Gathering a team of experts from AWS, Renova Cloud, and Cloud Kinetics, the program brings a complete picture of recruitment trends, office skills, AI-Ready mindset, and how to design enterprise-scale Cloud architectures.
