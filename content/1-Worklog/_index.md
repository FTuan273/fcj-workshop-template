**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [Introduction to AWS Cloud & EC2](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [Amazon S3 and IAM](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [Amazon VPC and Networking](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [Amazon RDS and CloudWatch](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [AWS Lambda and API Gateway](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [Docker and Amazon ECS](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [Infrastructure as Code and CI/CD](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [Event-driven Architecture and Amazon EventBridge](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [WebSocket, DynamoDB and Amazon SES](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [CloudFront, AWS WAF and Monitoring](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [Development and Integration of WebFood Project](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12:** [Testing, Optimization, and Finalization of Project](1.12-week12/)
