During my internship in the **First Cloud AI Journey (FCAJ)** program at AWS Vietnam, I participated in **05 community events** at the AWS office (26th Floor, Bitexco Tower). Each session helped me expand my Cloud/AI knowledge, soft skills, connect with the learner community, and improve my confident presentation skills.

### [Event 1 — Knowledge and experience sharing session (09/05/2026)](4.1-Event1/)

**Event Name:** Knowledge and experience sharing session  

**Date:** 09/05/2026  

**Location:** 26th Floor - AWS Vietnam Office (Bitexco Tower, 2 Hai Trieu Street, Saigon Ward, Ho Chi Minh City)  

**Role:** Attendee  

**Main Content:** Sharing sessions on knowledge and experience from seniors on effective learning methods, soft skills learned during the FCAJ program, how to use Proptimizer to optimize prompts, etc.

**Value Gained:** Listened to sharing, learned from seniors' experiences about knowledge and soft skills acquired during the FCAJ program.

### [Event 3 — GameDay: 8 Teams Battle (20/06/2026)](4.3-Event3/)

**Event Name:** Event GameDay 8 Đội Tranh Hùng (GameDay: 8 Teams Battle)  

**Date:** 20/06/2026   

**Location:** 26th Floor - AWS Vietnam Office (Bitexco Tower, 2 Hai Trieu Street, Saigon Ward, Ho Chi Minh City)  

**Role:** Attendee  

**Main Content:** AWS Knowledge Arena: The event atmosphere was "ignited" by a dramatic competition between 8 teams. Teams were divided into 4 knockout pairs, challenging their understanding of the AWS ecosystem. The spectacular score chases were not only to win the prestigious ticket to the next round but also to conquer extremely attractive "loot" from the organizers.

**Value Gained:** Systematized knowledge, identified gaps, and quickly reinforced knowledge about AWS services through real-world scenarios, trained combat mindset, improved analytical ability and quick reflexes under time pressure, broadened perspectives, and learned flexible problem-solving approaches from other teams.
