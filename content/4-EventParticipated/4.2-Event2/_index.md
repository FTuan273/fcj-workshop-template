- Share AWS, AI, and soft-skills knowledge within the **First Cloud Journey / FCAJ** community.
- Showcase hackathon projects and challenger products.
- Connect learners at the AWS Vietnam office.

- **Date:** **30/05/2026**

### Speakers / Sessions

#### 1. Huynh Thai Linh — Cloud Quest & Floci
*Level Up Your AWS Skills with Cloud Quest and Floci* — learning AWS via Cloud Quest, billing awareness, and local simulation tools (Floci vs LocalStack).

**Huynh Anh Khuong, Mai Quoc Anh, Nguyen Tran Minh Quan** shared their hackathon journey and demoed **SynthHunter** — an AI voice verification system that classifies speech as HUMAN / AI GENERATED / NEEDS REVIEW (XLS-R, Whisper, Fourier).

![SynthHunter — AI Voice Verification](/images/4-EventParticipated/4.2-Event2/03-synthhunter.jpg)

#### 3. Nguyen Thi Quynh Nhu — Why We Always Need Confidence
Soft skills: why confidence is a must-have for career and campus life; agenda covering self-doubt, the science behind the struggle, and everyday confidence “hacks”.
![Nguyen Thi Quynh Nhu — Confidence](/images/4-EventParticipated/4.2-Event2/04-quynh-nhu-confidence.jpg)

Introduced the **Tu Vi Dai Viet** product/idea (destiny insights / self-understanding), plus GenAI context and product vision/differentiation.

#### 5. Tran Minh Quan — The Hidden Iceberg of a Project: DevOps Before Disaster
A talk on the “hidden iceberg” of a project: adopting a **DevOps mindset before disaster** — surfacing delivery, operations, and collaboration risks early instead of reacting only after failure.

*A Familiar Enemy / The Iceberg of Procrastination* — reframing delay as fear (not laziness), with the closing message “GO BUILD — Starting today”.

### Takeaways & Application

- **AWS Learning Path:** Cloud Quest + billing awareness when practicing on a real account (relevant to **WebFood** deployment).
- **Hackathon & AI Project:** How to package a clear problem–solution (SynthHunter) and teamwork under time pressure.
- **Soft Skills:** Confidence and anti-procrastination habits — useful for long internship projects.
- **Product / DevOps Thinking:** Surface the project’s “hidden iceberg” early (DevOps before disaster), instead of waiting until failure.

### Event Experience

Community Day mixed diverse sessions: AWS learning, hackathon/AI demos, community products, and soft skills. The AWS Bitexco venue plus First Cloud Journey branding made the community atmosphere tangible.

> Overall, Event 2 broadened my view beyond the serverless lab — from AWS learning and AI projects to product thinking and soft skills.
