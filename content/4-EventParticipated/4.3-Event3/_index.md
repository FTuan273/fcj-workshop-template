### Key Highlights

- **Dramatic AWS Arena:** The head-to-head competition between 8 teams revolving around the AWS ecosystem created thrilling and fascinating score chases.
- **Dedicated Guidance from Hosts:** Mr. Long, Hoang Hieu, Thinh Nguyen, and Duy Khiem not only coordinated smoothly but also acted as knowledge transmitters. The direct translation and meticulous explanation of each answer turned every question into a highly accessible practical lesson.
- **Explosive Interaction:** The audience was not left behind thanks to the cleverness of the hosts who continuously gave hints and invited the audience to "rescue" the teams. This interaction, along with gifts from FCAJ, pushed the heat of the hall to a climax.

### Takeaways & Application

- **Systematizing Cloud Knowledge:** Identified my own knowledge gaps and reinforced core AWS services through practical hypothetical scenarios.
- **Training Combat Mindset:** Learned many troubleshooting tips and flexible, agile approaches from the in-depth explanations of the experts.
- **Practical Application:** Applied the mindset of designing network architecture and server infrastructure on AWS to optimize the deployment of ongoing software projects, typically the online food ordering application or internal management systems. The knowledge from the event helps shape how to configure the system to be more stable, secure, and load-bearing.

### Event Experience

The atmosphere at the 26th Floor - AWS Vietnam Office was truly explosive and full of energy. Watching the head-to-head matches brought suspense, while also stimulating reflex thinking as I, in the audience seat, was also drawn into reasoning for the answers under time pressure. The feeling of being immersed in a tech environment that is both fiercely competitive and extremely friendly and open was a wonderful experience.

> The GameDay event is not simply an entertaining playground but truly a miniature "training ground" to hone agile thinking and reinforce practical AWS knowledge. Through the event, I not only gained more professional values but also clearly felt the strong spirit of cohesion of the FCAJ community, thereby gaining tremendous motivation to continue developing on the path of pursuing Cloud technology.
